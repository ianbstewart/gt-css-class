 TO OUR SUBSCRIBERS. These our superiorswho have not paid for the current year—that is, fromJanuary 1st,1859, to January 1st, 1860,—are remindedof the standing rule by which their papers willbe discontinued after February 1st,1860, unless paymentfor the same be previously sent in. UBSCRIBERS. The Financial Committees, in calling attention tothe above standing rile, would also ask every subscriber,for the asks of the cause, as well as for thesake of the paper, to do what in him lies to extendthe circulation of the Liberator, at least to the extentof procuring each one an additional name for ourlist.
