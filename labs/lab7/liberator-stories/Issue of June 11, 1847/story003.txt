 BLACK ORATORS AND MINSTELS. Negro oratorylike negro minstrelsy, is getting to be an indispensableamusement. One Mr. Bibb, a runawayshare, is brought forward as a rival of Fred, Douglass,and has been 'enlighten in de folks ob Brook lays.'However, 'is said that Douglass' associationwith European ladies and gentlemen has renderedbeen entirely too clever to be easily outdone. Hewill remains 'the greatest nigger of them all'—Rice excepted.—Noah's Messenger. LACK RATORS AND INSTELS. Noah's Messenger.
