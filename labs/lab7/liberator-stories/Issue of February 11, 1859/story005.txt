 HEARING ON THE PETTION. We learn that theLegislative Committee on Federal Relations will givea hearing at the Stats House, on some day next weekyet to be determined, to the petition for a law prohibitingthe rendition of any person in Massachusettsunder the pretence, or on the claim, that said personis or has been a slave. Due notice of the hearingmay be expected in the daily papers. EARING ON THE ETTION.
